OPTIMUM STRATEGOS

Track the Fail; Trap the Competitors

Description: This is the event where you will have the opportunity to uncover the flaws of a given conglomerate/product/or scheme and present us with a strategy to reposition it.

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • A team can consist a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 2 members.
  • Mobiles are not allowed.
  • Malpractices would lead to the elimination of the team.
  • Usage of the internet is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision made by the judges will be the final.

YOU NEED:

PRELIMINARY ROUND

This round will be given a business case and a defined time frame to solve and submit their answer. All teams participating in this stage must submit a presentation of their separate solutions addressing the difficulties and concerns indicated in the case. All those who qualify this round will move to the next round.

FINAL ROUND

A business case will be presented to Round 2 participants, and they will have a specific window in which to solve it and submit their answer. The presentation should highlight their unique solutions to the case's issues and concerns, based on their innovative and strategic approach in making the product/conglomerate/ or scheme win. The foundation for evaluation will be the uniqueness, depth, and clarity of the participants' solutions.
