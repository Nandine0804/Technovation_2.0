VALORANT

Who's Gotta Defy the Limits now?

A gaming event where you can flex your gaming skills by taking out other teams continuously until you get that throne.

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • Each match has two teams of five players with 1 substitute player for each team( In case of unavailability of any players).
  • Each team can have five agents in the game to compete.
  • Matches have rounds, and the first team to win 13 rounds win the match.
  • Before a match begins, each player selects a character (called an Agent) with a unique set of abilities.
  • Winners And losers of the matches are then allocated to other set of matches to progress the tournament into finale.
