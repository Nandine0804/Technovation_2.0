SIMPLY BUSINESS

What if your creativity got wings?

Description: Join us for an exciting challenge where you will have the opportunity to unleash your creativity and develop a new product within a specific time frame using a set of materials provided to you. Construct a comprehensive business model canvas outlining the various elements of your product's commercial viability and stand a chance to win a prize!

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • Raw materials in the sandbox are available on first come, first served basis to the teams.
  • Teams are evaluated based on the business model canvas.
  • Electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited.
  • Malpractice will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision made by the judges will be the final.

YOU NEED:

PRELIMINARY ROUND

Give us an innovative value added product enriched by your creativity, with the help of raw materials we provide to you like paper, clay, etc. in a sandbox. Create a business model canvas for your product (guidance will be provided).

FINAL ROUND

Convince and sell your innovative product to the panel of customers. Each customer will have their own set of characteristics and the participants should analyse the customers and make a sales pitch.
