Description:An event where you can learn and unleash the limitless possibilities of doing monotonous work. RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • This is an individual event.
  • The evaluation will be based on bots, which are bug-free and efficient.
  • Participants are requested to bring their own laptops.
  • The decision made by the judges will be the final.

ROUND

The workshop gives an overall outline of how automation is carried out in the UiPath platform.The participants will be given various day-to-day usecases and scenarios that can be automated and will be asked to build bots on the usecases. The main objective is to understand the workflow and carry out the tasks.
